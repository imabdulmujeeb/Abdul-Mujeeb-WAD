Overview

As our first and flagship hotel, Pizza Fort Oriental, Lahore is as legendary as the city itself. With a Central location and five-star facilities, we are renowned for our sleek design, impressive service and exquisite blend of luxury, comfort and style.

As popular now as we’ve ever been, Pizza Fort Oriental, Lahore is an icon in its own right. Our restaurants is the best in Lahore, whilst our rooms, spa and facilities are truly outstanding.
